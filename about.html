my_motivation

Why Cloud & Cyber Security?

It all started with my home server. What began as a simple experiment quickly turned into a daily obsession — configuring Docker containers, setting up networking with Netbird, managing services through Cloudflare, and constantly finding new things to automate and improve.


I realized that what I was doing at home was exactly what professionals do in cloud infrastructure and cybersecurity. The more I learned at school, the more I could apply to my own server. And the more I tinkered at home, the better I understood what we covered in class. That feedback loop is what made me certain: this is what I want to do.
